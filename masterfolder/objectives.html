Our Objectives

  • 1.Field candidates in all elective posts and nominations slots during an election in the Republic of Kenya.
  • 2.Compete with other political parties democratically to win election and form government.
  • 3.Establish a government of the Republic of Kenya based on democratic and constitutional guides or principles of
    ,democracy and good governance founded on value of Unity,Justice, Fairness, Peace,Liberty,Love and Equity
    which ensures Transparency and accountability to the Electorate and all the Kenyan people.

  • 4.Engage in and provide quality leadership through accountability in matters of:
    • Political
    • economic
    • Social governance free from corruption, Nepotism, tribalism, racism and all vices which cause social,
      political and economic injustices.
  • 5.Promote democracy through free will of the people in the Republic of Kenya.
  • 6. Establish, enhance and entrench sound Economic institutions to ensure development.
  • 7.Promote and encourage coalition among political parties persuing similar objectives in Kenya.
  • 8.Protect and enhance participation of human Rights as articulated in the 2010 constitution of Kenya.
  • 9.Establish and put in place and practice systems of political governance aimed at eradicating all
    types/forms of corruption, tribalism, nepotism,racism,sectionalism, discrimination, oppression and
    exploitation of Kenyan people and foreigners.
  • 10.Improve the quality of life for all Kenyans through eradicating ignorance, poverty and diseases.
  • 11.Pursue, protect and promote Civil, political, Economic, Social and cultural rights and freedoms of the
    individual interlia,the right to life, right to human dignity and equality before the Law
  • 12.Work towards the independence of the Civil service through permission by the public employment act to run
    business inline with their proffession to help create employment and generate Revenue to the government.
  • 13.promote and protect the rights of workers by securing a justice return to work formula and remuneration gains
    in line with labour laws to improve living standards for all and ensure good welfare for all citizens.
  • 14. Protect the Rights of employers to get best output from services tendered by the employees.
  • 15.Respect and preserve our National Heritage, History, Cultural diversity,Historical sites,Archives
    to ensure reference in our past life to continue it to the future generations.
  • 16.Persue and promote full participation of women,youth, minorities, marginalized,
    persons leaving with disability (PLWD) and affirmative action.
  • 17.Promote genda equality and balance through providing equal opportunities in education,influence and ideologies,
    worker,social groups,Culture, Religion and politics.
  • 18.promote and defend National sovereignty and oppose all forms of adverse dominions , influence and ideologies.
    Pursue the provision and improvement of health, education and social welfare service for all Kenyans so as to
    raise the standards and quality of life of the people of Kenya.
  • 19.Pursue and formulate sound National population policies in order to improve the quality of life of all Kenyans
    inside and in the diaspora.
  • 20..Promote sound Environmental management and conservation policies to ensure sustainable development and responsible
    utilization of our natural resources and protect all public utilities, national wealth and natural resources for
    the betterment of lives of all Kenyans and posterity.
  • 21.Mobilize financial resources for the party by means including but not limited:
    • Registration fees and members contributions,
    • Borrowing from banks, saccos and individuals.
    • Rental and income generation activities
    • Invest any mones to banks and board of trustees.
    • Organizing funds drive and watts app groups.
  • 22.Promote,develop or devise schemes of awards , recognition or distinction of merity or honour and confer awards or
    marks of distinction to any person ,organization or institution that has rendered exemplary
