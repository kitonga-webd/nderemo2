vision:

To be an Exemplary Party to improve the livelihood of all Kenyans
and the Kenya state through establishing a prosperous,
industrialized and modern Nation in which all Kenyans enjoy Social,
Econmomic and Democratic Rights.

  1. To build a Social, Democratic country and a Nation in which all the 47 counties and the 45 communities will
    have a better way of life and feel protected as persons and cultural communities

  2. To Ensure Equal National Resources Distribution, Employment, Equal financial support, Equal security provision,
    Balanced food security provision and Economic improvements in all parts of the Republic

  3. To Promote National, Regional and International Unity and Relations to step up Trade, Social interactions, Culture,
    Games and Sports, Security, Religion, Education, Health and Economic Investments.

  6. To promote in full the key 15 party National pillars ref. Chapter A 1-15

pillars:

1. Protect the sovereignty of the constitution of the republic of Kenya and ensure democracy.
2. Promoting food security.

3. Promoting education.

4. Promoting healthy.
